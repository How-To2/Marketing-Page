Introduction:

We will help you, to help yourself to fix problems.

Welcome to our homepage of How-to. This site allows you to share your knowledge and make life easier for one another by helping people with basic day-to-day tasks. From tutorials to videos to step-by-step instructions, all will be provided on how to get through whatever task you want. Feel free to browse through anything that may help you save time or build your knowledge!

Our website allows users to create a profile and log in to their account. Users can create blog style posts that they can share, edit and remove. There is also a search function and ‘like button’ included. The posts with the most likes are displayed first. In the future we wish to have fully functioning How-To videos, a forum, instant messaging and a bookmark feature. This site takes inspiration from other business models such as Reddit, WikiHow and Google.

How-To uses Framework Libraries such as React, Redux, Less, Helmet, CORS, JsonWebtoken, and Express. We do not require an API or Apple Frameworks. We hope you enjoy the site as we put much effort into this creation. If you have any suggestions feel free to visit our ‘Contact Us’ page and send an email. We take our customers seriously!
